Dingoo A320

Overview

The Dingoo A320 is a chinese manufactured PMP by Shenzen Dingoo Digital. Out of the box, the handheld plays numerous emulators (NES, SNES, Mega-Drive, GBA, Neo Geo, CPS1, CPS2). The performance of some of the emulators is questionable. Also included are the standard set of PMP features.

Features

  • Built in 3D games included on handheld by default
  • Emulation support for NES, SNES, Mega-Drive, GBA, Neo-Geo, CPS1, and CPS2
  • Music player with support for MP3, WMA, APE, FLAC, and RA
    • Supports browsing by id3 tag metadata
    • Includes EQ and 3D sound processing
  • Includes FM Radio support
    • Background music player support for both radio and music files
  • Video player with support for RM, MP4, 3GP, AVI, ASF, WMV, MOV, FLV and MPEG
  • Voice recorder functionality with built in microphone
  • Picture viewer
  • Flash player (usability is questionable)
  • E-Book reader
  • TV-Out functionality, standard AV output

Hardware Specifications

These specs refer to the components of the earlier editions of the Dingoo A320, though minor changes have been made to the hardware over time.

See Also: Component Variations

CPU: Ingenics JZ4732 System-On-A-Chip MIPS processor @ 336Mhz (stable overclocks up to 470Mhz have been reported)
RAM: 2x Elpida UPD45128163G5-A75-9JF 128Mbit 133Mhz (total 256Mbit = 32MB of RAM)
Internal Memory: 1x Samsung K9LBG08U0M PCB0 (4GB NAND)
Expansion Slot: MiniSD Card, officially limited to 8GB (compatible with MicroSDHC via adapter, documented cases of 16GB support)
Display: 2.8" LCD, 320x240, 16M colors, ILI9325 or ILI9331_3
Battery: 3.7V 1700mAh Li-Ion, approximately 7 hours
Audio Output: Stereo speakers, 3.5mm Headphone jack
Input: D-Pad, 4 buttons, 2 shoulder buttons, internal microphone
Dimensions: 4.92in x 2.17in x 0.59in (125mm x 55.5mm x 14mm)
Weight: 11.53 oz. (110 g)

Dingoo A330

Overview

The A330 is essentially an A320 with 64MB of RAM, a more ergonomic shell with wrist strap loop, more appropriately placed shoulder buttons, a higher quality LCD screen, and the ability to be used with the "F-16" wireless controller (sold separately) while in tv-out mode. The A330's LCD has a clearer, more colorful image quality over the A320's, and is somewhat better with viewing angles.

Hardware Specifications

CPU: Ingenics JZ4740 System-On-a-Chip MIPS Processor @ 336Mhz (stable overclocks up to 470Mhz have been reported)
RAM: 2x Winbond W9825G6EH-6 IC SDRAM 256MB 166MHZ 54-TSOPII (total 512Mbit = 64MB of RAM)
Internal Memory: 1x Samsung K9LBG08U1M PCB0 (4GB NAND)
Expansion Slot: MiniSD Card, officially limited to 8GB (compatible with MicroSDHC via adapter, documented cases of 16GB support)
Display: 2.8" LCD, 320x240, 16M colors, ILI9331_3
Battery: 3.7V 1700mAh Li-Ion, approximately 7 hours
Audio Output: Stereo speakers, 3.5mm Headphone jack
Input: D-Pad, 4 buttons, 2 shoulder buttons, internal microphone
Wireless Receiver: Proprietary receiver board, unknown make/model, reported to operate in 2.4GHz range
